About the Role

Smart Working is hiring a Senior Software Engineer for our client, a UK-based hospitality technology organisation. This is a long-term role for one hire, suited to an experienced engineer with strong hands-on capability across TypeScript, Node.js, and Next.js.

The role requires 5+ years of experience in each of TypeScript, Node.js, and Next.js. 

Responsibilities

  • Design, develop, and maintain scalable web applications using TypeScript, Node.js, and Next.js

  • Build robust frontend and backend features, ensuring high performance, reliability, and maintainability

  • Collaborate with cross-functional stakeholders to understand requirements and deliver high-quality software solutions

  • Write clean, reusable, and well-tested code while following engineering best practices and coding standards

  • Participate in technical design discussions and contribute to the implementation of scalable application architectures

  • Review code, provide constructive feedback, and promote high standards of code quality across the team

  • Troubleshoot, debug, and resolve technical issues to ensure application stability and optimal performance

  • Contribute to the continuous improvement of development processes, code quality, and engineering practices

  • Work closely with other engineers to deliver software features within agreed timelines and project objectives

  • Stay up to date with modern web development practices and contribute to the ongoing evolution of the technology stack

Requirements

  • 5+ years of hands-on experience with TypeScript

  • 5+ years of hands-on experience with Node.js

  • 5+ years of hands-on experience with Next.js
