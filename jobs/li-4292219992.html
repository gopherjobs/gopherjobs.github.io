Description

Job Description Title : Senior GoLang Developer Location: Remote Any location in Mexico (from MTY, CDMX, GDL)

As a Senior GoLang Developer, you will be responsible for developing and maintaining complex enterprise applications. You will work with a team of engineers to deliver high-quality, scalable, and maintainable software solutions.

Mandatory

  • Bachelor’s degree in computer science or related field
  • 8+ years of hands-on experience in GoLang Development
  • Hands on experience working in gRPC and GCP
  • Good experience in design, development and unit testing
  • Should be able to work in short sprints, with short term planning and delivery in Agile working style
  • Ability to coordinate with multiple stakeholders and ensure one-time delivery of high-quality project deliverables.
  • Must have strong communication skills in English Language
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills

Nice To Have

  • Experience with Angular or other front-end frameworks
  • A proactive and results-oriented mindset
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • A strong work ethic
  • A passion for software development

Responsibilities Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop complex enterprise applications using GoLang on Google Cloud Platform
  • Build robust and scalable gRPC APIs.
  • Implement continuous integration and continuous delivery pipelines.
  • Collaborate with other team members to ensure timely delivery of projects.
