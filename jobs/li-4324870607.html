Job Description:

Strativ, a software company based in Sweden and Bangladesh, is seeking an ambitious and highly skilled Backend Developer who is passionate about advancing their career. We pride ourselves on fostering a collaborative and forward-thinking work environment. As we continue to expand, we are seeking a Golang developer to join our talented team. We are specifically looking for a candidate with a strong willingness to learn new technologies and an exceptional ability to communicate effectively in English.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Design, develop, and maintain the backend infrastructure of web applications using Go.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless integration across services and components.
  • Research and learn new tools and techniques, with a particular focus on Go best practices, libraries, and idiomatic patterns.
  • Show a strong willingness to adapt and master new technologies, frameworks, and methodologies, especially in cloud-native and microservice architectures.
  • Implement cutting-edge, scalable, and concurrent solutions to enhance our web applications and services.
  • Continuously improve code quality, performance, scalability, and maintainability through clean architecture and unit testing.
  • Provide technical support, troubleshoot issues, and proactively resolve performance bottlenecks or service disruptions.
  • Communicate effectively with both technical and non-technical stakeholders, demonstrating excellent English language skills.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
  • 4+ years of total professional experience in backend software development.
  • Minimum 2 years of strong production-level experience in Go (Golang).
  • Deep understanding of Go concurrency, goroutines, and channel patterns.
  • Proven experience writing unit tests and maintaining testable code using Go testing tools (testing, testify, etc.).
  • Solid understanding of Go best practices, idiomatic programming, and familiarity with popular Go libraries and patterns.
  • Experience with caching technologies such as Redis, and understanding of pub/sub patterns in distributed systems.
  • Hands-on experience working with Go templating (e.g., html/template, text/template) for internal tools or web rendering.
  • Strong knowledge of RESTful API design, backend service architecture, and asynchronous data processing.
  • Experience with relational databases like PostgreSQL or MySQL, and NoSQL databases like MongoDB.
  • Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines, containerization tools like Docker, and general DevOps workflows.
  • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure) for deploying and maintaining applications.
  • Proficiency in front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern JavaScript frameworks (e.g., React, Angular, or Vue.js) is a plus.
  • Excellent problem-solving, troubleshooting, and analytical skills.
  • Ability to work both independently and as part of a cross-functional team.
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills in English are a must.

  • Opportunity to work on an exciting and innovative project.

  • A ground-level opportunity to join a growing company early and accelerate your career
