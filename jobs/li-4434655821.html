Software Engineer - Go at BairesDev

As a Software Engineer specializing in Go, you will build and maintain high-concurrency services and microservices that power our core infrastructure and backend systems. You will connect complex business requirements with efficient, scalable code, ensuring robust performance and reliability across distributed environments.

What You'll Do:

- Design and develop high-performance backend services and APIs using Go.

- Implement efficient concurrency patterns to handle high-volume data and requests.

- Optimize and maintain database interactions using PostgreSQL for data integrity and performance.

- Containerize services using Docker and coordinate deployments within Kubernetes environments.

- Manage and scale cloud-based services across AWS or GCP infrastructure.

- Collaborate with engineering teams to ensure best practices in microservices architecture and API design.

What we are looking for:

- 4+ years of experience in Software Engineering with a focus on Go.

- Strong expertise in building high-concurrency backend services and microservices.

- Proficiency in developing and consuming REST and gRPC APIs.

- Solid experience with PostgreSQL and understanding of concurrency patterns.

- Hands-on familiarity with Docker, Kubernetes, and cloud platforms like AWS or GCP.

- Advanced proficiency in English.
