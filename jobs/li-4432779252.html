Your Role in Keeping the Future in Safe Hands… As a Go Backend Cloud Native Engineer for the Hospitality Global solutions division of ASSA ABLOY, you will have the opportunity to work as team member within the R&D department and reporting the results to the R&D Team.

Reporting directly to the Chief Technical Officer, you will be responsible and work mainly on Cloud native applications and transformation process.

What You Will Do…

  • Design and execute the transition from a modular monolith architecture to a cloud-native microservices architecture.
  • Define the strategy for decomposing the monolith into independent services.
  • Design communication patterns between services (event-driven, synchronous and asynchronous APIs).
  • Build the technical foundation of the new Go backend platform (services, standards, and shared structure).
  • Establish architectural standards for distributed systems (resilience, consistency, observability).
  • Implement the execution foundation on Kubernetes and continuous deployment practices.
  • Integrate messaging systems to decouple domains and enable asynchronous processing.
  • Define and introduce observability practices from the design stage (structured logs, metrics, distributed tracing).
  • Act as a technical reference for cloud-native architecture decisions within the backend team.

What We Expect Of You…

  • At least 2 years of experience in a cloud-native technical/development environment or demonstrable skills – we care about what you can do, not just your resume.
  • Previous experience in system integration projects (highly valued).
  • Backend development in Go for production cloud native systems.
  • Go performance tuning (profiling and optimization)
  • Design of microservices and APIs (REST/gRPC).
  • Knowledge of API security practices: Authentication, authorization, secure token handling (OAuth, JWT, MD5)
  • Distributed systems with messaging (Kafka/RabbitMQ/NATS).
  • Containerization with Docker.
  • Deployment and operations of services in Kubernetes.
  • Production incident handling and debugging of distributed systems.
  • DevOps-oriented thinking.
  • Analytical thinking and strong attention to details.
  • High or upper-intermediate level of spoken and written English and Spanish.
  • Good skills to have remote meetings in English/Spanish.
  • You also have personal and professional skills.
  • You are ideally located in the Barcelona metropolitan area (or surroundings). Not excluding candidates from other regions in Spain.
