About the Role

As a Full-Stack Software Developer, you will play a pivotal role in driving the evolution of our industry-leading out-of-home media platforms by bridging the gap between robust backend architectures and sleek, responsive user interfaces. This role is designed for a developer who thrives on clean code, innovation, values strong engineering practices, and wants to see their work impact millions of viewers globally.

What to Expect

You will join our Amsterdam-based OutMoove team at Broadsign to build and optimize end-to-end features for our programmatic digital out-of-home Demand-Side Platform

(DSP). You will develop powerful backend services using Go and craft intuitive frontend experiences

with Angular to provide seamless campaign management workflows for major media agencies worldwide while championing high-quality development methodologies.

What you need to perform this job

  • 5-10 years of professional software development experience
  • Expertise with Go for backend services and Angular for modern frontend development
  • Deep commitment to clean code principles
  • Experience with automated testing is an asset
  • Fluency in English is mandatory
  • Excellent problem-solving abilities, an innovation-first mindset, and a collaborative approach that elevates the who le team.
