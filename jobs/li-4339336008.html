Key Responsibilities

Manual Testing – Analyze requirements, user stories, and acceptance criteria to design test strategy and test scenarios.

– Create and execute Test Cases, Test Suites, and Test Data across web, mobile, API, and backend.

– Perform functional testing, regression testing, smoke/sanity testing, compatibility testing, and UAT support.

– Log defects with clear evidence using Jira/Azure DevOps.

Automation Testing (Preferred) – Participate in building automation test frameworks (Selenium, Playwright, Cypress, Appium).

– Automate regression suites and integrate automation into CI/CD pipelines.

QA Process & Collaboration – Collaborate with BA, Developer, PM to clarify requirements.

– Join sprint ceremonies and maintain QA documentation.

– Conduct root-cause analysis and propose improvements.

Quality Assurance & Improvements – Identify requirement gaps and strengthen QA best practices.

Required Skills & Qualifications Technical Skills (Manual) – 3+ years of experience in manual testing.

– Strong understanding of SDLC/STLC, API testing, SQL basics, Agile methodology.

Technical Skills (Automation – Preferred) – Basic/intermediate skills in one programming language (JavaScript/Java/C#/Python).

– Experience with Selenium, Playwright, or Cypress.

– Knowledge of POM and automation frameworks.

– CI/CD experience is a plus.

Soft Skills – Strong communication, teamwork, analytical thinking.

– English reading & writing.

Nice-to-have – Performance testing (JMeter, k6)

– Mobile testing (Appium)

– QA experience in manufacturing, retail, fintech, AI systems

– Cloud knowledge (Azure/AWS)
