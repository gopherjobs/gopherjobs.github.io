Role Overview:

We are hiring for one of our clients, seeking a Software Engineer (Human Data Platforms) to work on a full-time basis. The role involves designing, developing, and maintaining scalable data platforms that process and analyze human-generated datasets. The position requires expertise in handling large-scale data infrastructure while ensuring performance, reliability, and security.

Key Responsibilities:

• Design, build, and optimize data pipelines to process human-generated datasets at scale.

• Develop and maintain backend services that support real-time and batch data processing.

• Collaborate with data scientists and analysts to ensure data integrity and accessibility.

• Implement monitoring, logging, and alerting systems to maintain platform reliability.

• Contribute to architectural decisions that enhance scalability and performance of data systems.

Required Skills & Qualifications:

• Proficiency in Python, Java, or Go for backend development.

• Experience with distributed systems, including Kafka, Spark, or similar technologies.

• Knowledge of cloud platforms such as AWS, GCP, or Azure for data infrastructure.

• Familiarity with SQL and NoSQL databases for data storage and retrieval.

• Understanding of data governance, security, and compliance best practices.

• Ability to design and optimize data pipelines for performance and scalability.

• Experience with containerization and orchestration tools like Docker and Kubernetes.

• Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail in debugging complex systems.
