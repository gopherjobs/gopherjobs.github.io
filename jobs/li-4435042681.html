About Us

31 Green is a fast-growing and innovative organization building the digital infrastructure that powers next-generation HealthTech solutions. We are committed to transforming health outcomes by combining advanced AI, deep learning, and real-time movement analysis. Our mission is to reshape the future of digital health by creating technology that enhances patient outcomes without compromising trust, accessibility, or privacy.

As part of our continued scale-up, we are hiring a skilled DevOps Engineer to support core

application environments, deployment infrastructure, and CI/CD automation across cloud platforms.

Role Overview

As a DevOps Engineer at 31 Green, you will work closely with engineering teams to build, maintain, and optimize cloud-based environments across multiple deployments. You will take ownership of CI/CD pipelines, monitoring systems, infrastructure automation, and environment stability ensuring fast, reliable, and secure product delivery. This role requires strong experience in AWS and DevOps practices, a passion for automation, and a problem-solving mindset.

Key Responsibilities

• Configure and maintain AWS-based infrastructure.

• Build, enhance, and optimize CI/CD pipelines.

• Develop technical solutions using DevOps best practices.

• Write custom scripts and tooling using Python / Golang / Bash.

• Participate in architecture discussions and development planning.

• Translate loosely defined requirements into actionable implementations.

• Use open-source tools and technologies to solve specific infrastructure needs.

• Collaborate with engineering teams to address deployment, scaling, and security issues.

• Conduct incremental testing across code, deployment, and automation processes to reduce

errors.

• Monitor production environments and perform upgrades to ensure stability and reliability.

Skills & Qualifications

• Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or a related field.

• 3–5+ years of experience as a Production/DevOps Engineer (or similar role).

• Hands-on experience with AWS.

• Proficiency in at least one scripting language: Python, Golang, Bash, etc.

• Experience working on open-source platforms.

• Proven experience in infrastructure automation and configuration management

(Kubernetes, Ansible, Docker).

• Knowledge of virtualization environments.

• Experience with Git or other distributed version control systems.

• Familiarity with SQL and NoSQL databases — ideally ElasticSearch or Cassandra.

• Ability to work collaboratively in an Agile environment with cross-functional teams.

• Strong understanding of DevOps security and disaster recovery practices.
