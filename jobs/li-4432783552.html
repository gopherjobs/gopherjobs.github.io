Description

Chevron Global Business Services (GBS), located in Buenos Aires (Puerto Madero), Argentina, is accepting online applications for the position of Optimization Software Engineer. Successful candidates will join the IT Company which is part of a successful multifunction service center with a workforce of more than 1800 employees delivering business services and solutions across the globe.

At Chevron, we’re looking for motivated Software Engineers to deliver innovative digital solutions through design, development, deployment, and ongoing support. In this role, you’ll implement new backend services within an established cloud architecture, partnering with product and engineering teams in an agile environment to build reusable components and services. Successful candidates apply best practices in software engineering to ensure quality and maintainability, with a focus on modularity, scalability, observability, and integration while building secure, production ready systems that support business critical workflows

Responsibilities

  • Build and enhance backend Python services, owning the full software development lifecycle end-to-end (design, implement, deploy, maintain, deprecate).
  • Develop and maintain cloud native solutions leveraging Azure services such as Azure Functions, Azure Storage/Blob, and Azure Service Bus messaging.
  • Collaborate with cross functional teams to gather requirements, translate them into technical designs, and deliver production ready solutions.
  • Troubleshoot software issues to identify root causes, propose and implement fixes, and drive preventative improvements. Create and maintain clear technical documentation to support reliable development, deployment, and user support.
  • Strong business acumen and ability to understand and address business needs.
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills.
  • Participate in code reviews and contribute to continuous improvement of engineering standards and practices.
  • Improve reliability, scalability, and developer experience through tooling, automation, and iterative refinements.
  • Stay current with advancements in cloud native development and modern engineering workflows.

Requirements

  • 3+ years of professional backend software engineering experience with Python.
  • Experience owning the full software lifecycle for production software (design, implement, deploy, maintain, and deprecate).
  • Experience building and operating solutions on major cloud platforms such as Microsoft Azure, Amazon Web Services (AWS), or Google Cloud Platform (GCP).
  • Git proficiency including pull requests, branching strategies, conflict resolution, and tagging/releases.
  • Familiarity with CI/CD pipeline development and maintenance.
  • Bachelor’s or master’s degree in computer science, Computer Engineering, or a related technical discipline.
  • Strong level of English and solid communication skills.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience improving observability and operability (structured logging, metrics/monitoring, deployment validation/rollback practices).
  • Familiarity with agentic coding tools and demonstrated judgment in using them safely and effectively.
  • Experience in the oil and gas industry or a related field.
  • Understanding of supply chain management and logistics.
