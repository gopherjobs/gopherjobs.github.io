Role Overview:

We are hiring for one of our clients, seeking a Backend Engineer to work on a contract basis. The role involves designing, building, and maintaining scalable backend systems to support high-traffic applications. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver robust solutions that meet performance and reliability requirements.

Key Responsibilities:

• Design, develop, and optimize backend services using modern programming languages and frameworks.

• Implement and maintain APIs, databases, and microservices architecture.

• Troubleshoot and resolve performance bottlenecks, bugs, and system failures.

• Write clean, maintainable, and well-documented code following best practices.

• Participate in code reviews, testing, and deployment processes to ensure high-quality deliverables.

Required Skills & Qualifications:

• Proficiency in backend programming languages such as Python, Java, or Go.

• Experience with RESTful APIs, GraphQL, or similar web service technologies.

• Strong understanding of database design, SQL, and NoSQL systems.

• Familiarity with cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud.

• Knowledge of containerization and orchestration tools such as Docker and Kubernetes.

• Experience with CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices.

• Ability to write unit, integration, and end-to-end tests.

• Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

More About the Opportunity:

This role offers a unique opportunity to work with a global leader in the Technology, Information and Internet industry, contributing to the development of scalable backend systems that power critical applications. You will be part of a collaborative team focused on innovation and technical excellence.
