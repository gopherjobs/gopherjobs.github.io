Description

Company Description X-42 is building the next-generation operating system for hospitality, offering a unified cloud-native platform for point of sale, payments, inventory, workforce management, payroll, reservations, analytics, and AI. By streamlining critical functions into one ecosystem, X-42 enables restaurants, cafés, hotels, and hospitality groups to operate more efficiently and focus on guest experiences. The company is headquartered in New York City, NY, and is dedicated to reducing operational complexity through automation, real-time data, and intelligent workflows. X-42 is creating the infrastructure that powers the future of hospitality, giving businesses tools that scale with their growth and evolving needs.

Role Description The Site Reliability Engineer is a full-time, remote role responsible for ensuring the reliability, performance, and scalability of X-42’s cloud-native hospitality platform. This role involves designing and maintaining production infrastructure, implementing monitoring and alerting, and managing incident response to maintain high availability and service quality. The Site Reliability Engineer will collaborate with software engineers to improve system architecture, automate deployment pipelines, and enhance observability across services. Day-to-day tasks include troubleshooting complex system issues, optimizing resource usage, refining reliability standards, and contributing to documentation and best practices for operations.

Qualifications

  • Candidates should possess strong skills in Site Reliability Engineering, including designing for resiliency, scalability, and high availability.
  • Candidates should possess hands-on Troubleshooting skills for complex distributed systems and production incidents.
  • Candidates should possess Software Development skills, such as scripting and programming for automation, tooling, and infrastructure-as-code. Especially TERRAFORM.
  • Candidates should possess System Administration skills, including managing Linux-based environments, networking fundamentals, and security best practices.
  • Some level of backend code and understanding of Node JS.
  • Candidates should possess Infrastructure skills, such as experience with cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, GCP, Azure), containers and orchestration (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes), and CI/CD pipelines.
  • Experience with monitoring and logging tools (e.g., Prometheus, Grafana, ELK, OpenTelemetry) and incident management processes.
  • Strong communication and collaboration abilities, with a proactive approach to improving reliability and operational efficiency.
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field, or equivalent practical experience.
