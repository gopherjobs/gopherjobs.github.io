Job Objective

Design and govern the backend architecture of distributed systems, ensuring scalability, resilience, and efficiency in data and process management.

Your role

Design distributed architectures (microservices, modular monoliths)Define backend development standardsSelect technologies (Java, .NET, Node.js, Python, etc.)Design APIs (REST, GraphQL, gRPC)Implement scalability strategies (horizontal/vertical)Define design patterns (DDD, CQRS, Event Sourcing)Manage databases (SQL and NoSQL)Design security mechanisms (authentication, authorization, encryption)Ensure monitoring, logging, and observabilityCollaborate on CI/CD pipelines

Your profile

  • 7+ years in backend development, 2+ in architecture

  • Proficiency in backend languages (Java, C#, Node.js, Python, Go)
  • Experience with APIs and event-driven systems (Kafka, RabbitMQ)
  • Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Experience with relational and NoSQL databases
  • Knowledge of advanced design patterns

Nice to Have

  • Knowledge of serverless architectures
