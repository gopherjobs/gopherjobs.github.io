Company Description:

VinSmart Future (VSF) is the leading technology company within the Vingroup Corporation, formed by the merger of the group's entire technology ecosystem, including VinApp, VinIT, VinBigdata, and other tech units. As a core driver of Vingroup's future growth, VSF is at the forefront of technological development, with artificial intelligence (AI) as its foundation. With a talented team of nearly 4,000 local and international technology experts, VSF focuses on creating high-utility technologies that enhance lives and connect data, models, and infrastructure to unlock new possibilities.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design, build, and operate scalable backend services and APIs.
  • Model and optimize data storage across SQL and NoSQL systems.
  • Deploy and run services on AWS with an eye on cost, performance, and reliability.
  • Partner across Frontend, Mobile, DevOps, and Product to deliver features end-to-end.
  • Bake security into everything — from data handling to API design.
  • Raise the bar through code reviews, technical design, and mentoring.

Qualifications:

  • 5+ years of backend engineering experience.
  • Strong proficiency in Go (primary), Rust or Java (optional).
  • Strong SQL skills — schema design, query optimization, and data integrity.
  • Hands-on experience building and deploying on AWS.
  • Solid security fundamentals (secure coding, auth, data protection).
  • Proven collaboration across teams (FE, Mobile, DevOps, Product) and a healthy, accountable team culture.

Nice to have

  • Experience with NoSQL databases (e.g., MongoDB, Redis, DynamoDB).
  • Built or scaled large-scale products (~1M+ concurrent users).
  • A big-data mindset — comfort with high-volume data pipelines and analytics thinking.
